Saeed Ezatolahi is an Iranian professional footballer born on 22 December 1996 in Ardabil, Iran. He primarily plays as a midfielder and is known for his strong physical presence and technical skills on the ball.Ezatolahi began his youth career at the Iranian club Sepahan before moving to the youth system of the English club Manchester United in 2013.
